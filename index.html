English Alphabets

An apple is a round, edible fruit that's usually red, yellow, or green and 2–4 inches (5–10 centimeters) wide. It's a pome fruit that grows on apple trees (genus Malus) in the rose family and is native to temperate zones of both hemispheres.

A ball is a round or roundish object that can be thrown, hit, or kicked in games and sports. Balls can be made of many materials, including leather, rubber, synthetics, and animal parts.

Cats have very sweet features. It has two beautiful eyes, adorably tiny paws, sharp claws, and two perky ears which are very sensitive to sounds.

Dogs are four-legged mammals that are part of the Canidae family and the Carnivora order. Their scientific name is Canis lupus familiaris and they are a subspecies of the gray wolf.

Elephants are the largest land mammals on earth and have distinctly massive bodies, large ears, and long trunks. They use their trunks to pick up objects, trumpet warnings, greet other elephants, or suck up water for drinking or bathing, among other uses.

Fish are aquatic vertebrate animals that have gills but lack limbs with digits, like fingers or toes. Recall that vertebrates are animals with internal backbones. Most fish are streamlined in their general body form.
